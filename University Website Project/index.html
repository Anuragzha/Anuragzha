Welcome to Website

Baddi University Of Emerging Science And Technology is a leading Engineering College in Baddi, Dist.-Solan. The college is located at Makhnumajra, Baddi Industrial Area, In Himachal Pradesh.

Baddi University of Emerging Sciences and Technologies (BUEST), commonly known as Baddi University was established in 2002 and recognized as a university in 2009. Spread over 40 acres of land, the institute has built up an impressive structure for academics with project-based learning and excellent placements.
